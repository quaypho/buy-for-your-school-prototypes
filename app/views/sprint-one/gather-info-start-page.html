Gather information on what you're buying

Overview

This tool will help you to gather and define your requirements for the goods and services you want to buy for your school. The questions will prompt you to think about and provide answers to key considerations.

Your answers will be used to build a draft specification that:

  • will include recommended sections covering boiler plate standards and regulations
  • can be downloaded as an editable document
  • can be used to for the tender process

Before you start

You will need:

  • to know who you want to keep informed about this procurement and their email addresses
  • an understanding of what you're trying to buy
    • when you need it by
    • who it's for
    • what it needs to do
    • any specical or unique requirements
  • information from the current contract

It will take around 20 minutes to complete the process
